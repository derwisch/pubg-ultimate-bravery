About Battleground Bravery

Battleground Bravery aims to be a custom fun gamemode for PLAYERUNKNOWN'S BATTLEGROUNDS inspired by the custom gamemode Ultimate Bravery for league of legends.

Like Ultimate Bravery it aims to bring some fresh wind to the game by forcing you to play weapons you'd usually just skip and land at places you wouldn't land normally.

Rules

To play Battleground Bravery just select the map you are playing and follow two simple rules:

  • Land in the area that is marked on the map
  • You are only allowed to pick up the weapons you rolled
A few suggestions if you want to change the rules a bit:
  • You are allowed to pick up the nearest weapon if an enemy lands nearby
  • You may exchange one weapon with a weapon from a carepackage
  • You are only allowed to drive the first vehicle you see
